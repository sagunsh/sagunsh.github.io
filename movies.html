Keeping a list of movies so that I don't forget

  • Shawshank Redemption - A banker goes to jail for a crime he didn't commit, escapes prison later with a lot of money.
  • Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind - Two people fall in love, breakup, erases their memory to forget each other and fall in love again.
  • Taxi Driver - Ex-marine works as a taxi driver, gets rejected by a girl and becomes a hero accidentally.
  • Before Sunrise - Two young people meet on train, started talking and hangout together until the end of movie.
  • Lord of the Rings - People fighting, killing and dying for a ring
  • The Silence of the Lambs - FBI rookie officer meets a serial killer to help her find another serial killer.
    • The Secret Life of Walter Mitty - A man with boring life day-dream about action, love and other exciting things.
  • The Prestige - Two magicians competing to become the best.
  • Dead Poets Society - Teenage boys studying private school, one commit suicide because his dad won't let him follow his dream.

  • October Sky - School kids from a coal mining town builds rocket.
  • 12 Angry Men - 12 men who are assigned jury duty, 1 convinces the remaining 11 other.
